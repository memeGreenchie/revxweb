Decentralized AI Agents. Transparent Revenue Sharing. Community-Driven Growth.


RevX is building a decentralized ecosystem where AI agents autonomously generate value and that value is transparently shared with the community through blockchain technology.

Our mission is to democratize access to AI-powered services and revenue streams. Through a network of AI agents and on-chain revenue sharing mechanisms, anyone can participate in and benefit from the growing AI economy.

Driven by transparency, fairness, and community governance, RevX is creating a new paradigm for value creation and distribution in the Web3 era.
